General Info

CribMD is a digital Doctor on Demand platform that whose goal is to provide medical care at a very affordable rate for the rich and the poor alike. Our platform makes it possible for anyone anywhere to access medical care at any time without barrier of distance. You can either request for a home visit from a doctor or consult a Doctor through our online HD video calling services

CribMD makes it possible for a patient to choose from a list of doctors registered with CribMD and then choose whether to request a house visit or a video call with the doctor.
Patients make payment for services on a subscription basis; the patients pay a fixed amount of money in a month and they are covered for every doctor visit for the month.
Doctors on the other hand get paid for the number of patients they attend to.
Non Urgent Request:
  1. The doctor logs into his dashboard on CribMD to schedule the times that he is available during the week.
  2. The patient comes to CribMD to book a doctor
  3. We show the patients a list of available doctors
  4. Patient schedules appointment with any available doctor
  5. Doctor sees the patient at their Crib/Home or through telemedicine
  6. Doctor writes your prescription and sends it to a pharmacy if there is a prescription.
  7. Patient rates the service received from the doctor.
  8. Care team and doctor perform follow up as needed

Urgent / Emergency:
  1. The patient logs into their CribMD account to book a doctor.
  2. The patient specifies that he/she needs a doctor urgently.
  3. We send message to every available doctor in the system.
  4. The first doctor to accept the request attends to the patient and the request is cancelled for every other doctor.
  5. Doctor writes your prescription and sends it to a pharmacy if there is a prescription.
  6. Patient rates the service received from the doctor.
  7. Care team and doctor perform follow up as needed
