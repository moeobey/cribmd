How it works

Create an Organisation

If you don’t already have an account with CribMD, then you first need to create an account with CribMD as you automatically become the overall admin of the Organisation. Afterwards, you create an organisation using the name of your company.

Purchase slots for your members

Secondly, you then need to purchase slots for the members that you’ll add to your organisation. You can do this easily by choosing your preferred plan between Corporate Classic and Corporate Executive and you purchase slots available within those plans. Please note that you can have the two plans within your organisation, depending on how you want to attribute it to your team members.

Add Members to start using the Plan

We have gotten to the final step. All you need do now is add the team members that you want to enjoy your Corporate Plan to the purchased slots and voila! You’re good to start enjoying all the benefits of the corporate plan.
